About Us

Since September 2022, CE Lighting Decor Supply Opc has been a beacon of brilliance in the lighting industry, illuminating homes, businesses, and lives throughout the Philippines. Founded by experienced visionaries with two decades of expertise, our journey began with a simple yet profound goal: to provide exceptional lighting solutions that not only illuminate spaces but also enhance their aesthetic appeal. Fueled by unwavering determination, we embraced challenges as opportunities to innovate, adapt to evolving trends, and expand our product range to cater to our valued customers' changing needs.

Today, we proudly stand as a trusted supplier of renowned lighting brands in the Philippines, earning the trust of homeowners, businesses, architects, and interior designers. As we move forward, CE Lighting Decor Supply Opc remains dedicated to lighting up the future with innovative and energy-efficient solutions that contribute to a sustainable tomorrow. Our Mission and Vision

Our mission at CE Lighting Decor Supply Opc is to brighten lives by providing
the most innovative, energy-efficient, and aesthetically pleasing lighting solutions for every Pilipinos.
We are committed to enhancing spaces, creating ambiance, and contributing to a sustainable and greener
future through our lighting expertise.

Our vision is to be the leading lighting provider in the Philippines and beyond,setting
the standard for quality, innovation, and customer service in the industry. We aim to illuminate
every corner of the Philippines, making it a brighter and more beautiful and greener place
one light at a time
